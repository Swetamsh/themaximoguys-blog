# LinkedIn Post Copy Templates

How to generate LinkedIn post text to accompany each infographic type.

## Universal Structure

Every LinkedIn post follows this structure:

```
[HOOK — 1-2 lines that stop the scroll]

[BODY — 3-8 lines of value, formatted for mobile scanning]

[CTA — drive engagement: save, share, comment, follow]

[HASHTAGS — 3-5 relevant, mix broad + niche]
```

**Character limit:** LinkedIn posts truncate at ~210 characters on mobile feed. The HOOK must be complete before that cutoff.

**Formatting rules:**
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Line breaks between every thought
- Use → arrows instead of bullets (LinkedIn doesn't render bullets well)
- Bold sparingly (LinkedIn supports **bold** in some clients)
- No emojis in the first line (looks spammy) — emojis OK in body/CTA

## Hashtag Strategy

**Always include 3-5 hashtags. Mix these tiers:**

| Tier | Reach | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Broad** (1-2) | High reach, general audience | #AssetManagement, #DigitalTransformation, #Maintenance |
| **Platform** (1-2) | Mid reach, platform-specific | #Maximo, #SAPEAM, #HxGNEAM, #IBMMaximo |
| **Niche** (1-2) | Low reach, highly targeted | #MaximoTips, #EAMStrategy, #CMMSvsEAM, #MaximoMobile |

**Never use:** #Trending, #Viral, #FollowMe, or more than 5 hashtags (looks spammy).

---

## Post Timing Guidance

- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM (poster's local timezone)
- **Avoid:** Weekends, Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

## Engagement Hooks That Work

**Proven patterns for EAM/Maximo/technical LinkedIn:**
- "I've seen [X] organizations get this wrong."
- "The [topic] cheat sheet I wish I had on Day 1."
- "Stop [doing the wrong thing]. Start [doing the right thing]."
- "[Specific number] [things] that will [specific benefit]."
- "[Tool/Concept] is not [what people think]. Here's what it actually is."
- "I watched a [role] spend [time] doing [thing] that takes [shortcut]."
- "If your [system] still [bad practice], we need to talk."
