# TheMaximoGuys X/Twitter Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Handle:** @TheMaximoGuys
**Relevance:** 6/10
**Priority:** Tier 2 — Start Month 2

---

## Why X/Twitter (Honest Assessment)

- Manufacturing engagement dropped to **23%** of 2020 levels
- Median engagement rate: **0.015%** (very low)
- BUT: IBM product teams, tech analysts, and industry journalists are still active
- Good for real-time commentary on IBM announcements and conference live-tweeting
- **Low effort, low reward** — but worth maintaining for networking

---

## Content Strategy

| Format | Cadence | Content |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Threads** | 2x/week | Compress blog posts into 5-8 tweet threads |
| **Single tweets** | Daily | Quick tips, reactions to IBM news, industry commentary |
| **Live-tweeting** | Events | MaximoWorld, IBM TechXchange, IBM Think |

---

## Hashtags

1-2 per tweet: `#IBMMaximo #EAM #MAS #PredictiveMaintenance #CMMS`

---

## Key Insight

X is a **networking** platform for Maximo, not a content distribution platform. Use it to engage with IBM employees, industry analysts, and conference speakers. The content play is secondary.
