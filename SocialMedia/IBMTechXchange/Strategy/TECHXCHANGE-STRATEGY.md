## Why IBM TechXchange

- **THE home base** for Maximo practitioners — highest concentration anywhere online
- IBM employees and product managers are active (direct relationship building)
- UK & Ireland MUG has 800+ members; regional MUGs meet regularly
- Content here directly influences buyers during evaluation
- User group presentations are a gateway to consulting relationships

## Strategy

- Create a TheMaximoGuys profile/presence
- Answer technical questions regularly
- Share blog posts as resources when relevant
- Participate in Maximo User Group discussions
- Attend and contribute to virtual events

- MaxTECH (technical user group)

## Important Note

This is NOT a social media play — it's a **community credibility play**. The ROI shows up as trust across all other platforms. Your primary content lives on owned properties (blog, newsletter, YouTube). TechXchange is a distribution channel and relationship builder.
