# TheMaximoGuys YouTube Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Channel:** youtube.com/@themaximoguys
**Relevance:** 9/10
**Priority:** Tier 1 — Start Immediately

---

## Why YouTube

- YouTube is the **#2 search engine** — Maximo tutorial content ranks easily due to almost zero competition
- Evergreen content compounds — a good tutorial from 2026 still drives traffic in 2028
- Almost **no competition** in the Maximo-specific tutorial space
- Maximo Secrets has a YouTube presence but posts infrequently
- Banetti offers training videos but they're course-formatted, not discoverable
