## Strategy: Syndication (Not Duplication)

| Platform | Audience | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Medium** | General tech, executives | High-level thought leadership, migration strategy |
| **dev.to** | Developers, practitioners | API guides, automation scripts, integration patterns |
| **Hashnode** | Developer community | Technical deep-dives, code examples |

---

## How to Syndicate

1. Publish original on themaximoguys.com blog
2. Wait 1-2 weeks
3. Republish on Medium/dev.to with **canonical URL** pointing to your blog (preserves SEO)
4. Optimize the title for each platform's audience

---

## Cadence

- 2 articles/month on Medium
- 1 article/month on dev.to
