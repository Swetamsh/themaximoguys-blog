# LinkedIn Poll Template

## Format Rules
- **Question length:** Max 140 characters
- **Options:** 2-4 choices (4 options get the most engagement)
- **Duration:** 1 week (default, highest participation)
- **Follow-up:** Always comment with your own answer + reasoning after posting

## Poll Structure

```
Caption:
[1-2 sentences setting context for why this poll matters]

[The question — keep it specific to the Maximo/EAM audience]

Poll Question: [140 chars max]
Option 1: [25 chars max]
Option 2: [25 chars max]
Option 3: [25 chars max]
Option 4: [25 chars max]

#IBMMaximo #TheMaximoGuys #[Topic hashtag]
```

## Follow-up Comment Template
```
My take: [Option X].

Here's why: [2-3 sentences explaining your reasoning]

What about you? Drop your reasoning below — I want to hear
from teams who've been through this.
```
