# LinkedIn Text Post Templates

## Format Rules
- **Length:** 150-300 words (sweet spot for engagement)
- **Line breaks:** Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- **Hook:** First 2 lines must be compelling (that's all that shows before "...see more")
- **Hashtags:** 3-4 at the END of the post
- **No external links in the post body** — put links in comments (algorithm penalty for links)

---

## Template 1: Hot Take / Opinion

```
[Controversial or surprising statement — 1 line]

Here's why:

[3-5 short paragraphs explaining your position]

[Paragraph 1 — the context]
[Paragraph 2 — the evidence]
[Paragraph 3 — the implication]
[Paragraph 4 — what to do about it]

Agree? Disagree? Tell me in the comments.

#IBMMaximo #[Topic hashtag] #TheMaximoGuys
```

**Example:**
```
Stop customizing Maximo the old way. Seriously.

I've seen teams spend 6 months building custom MBOs
that MAS already handles out of the box.

The platform changed. Your approach needs to change too.

In MAS, customization means:
→ Automation scripts (not Java classes)
→ REST APIs (not MIF endpoints)
→ Configuration (not code)

The teams that thrive on MAS are the ones
that unlearn their Maximo 7.6 habits first.

What's the hardest habit to break?

#IBMMaximo #MAS #TheMaximoGuys
```
