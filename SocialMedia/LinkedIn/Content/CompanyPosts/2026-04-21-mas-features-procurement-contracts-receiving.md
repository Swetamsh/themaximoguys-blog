## Post Copy

```
Ten contract types. Five cost accounting methods. Zero Purchasing RBAs.

MAS 9 procurement is comprehensive and powerful -- but the modernization is incomplete.

-> Procurement flows from Desktop Requisitions through PRs to POs, with RFQ-based multi-vendor comparison and change orders
-> Ten contract types: Purchase, Blanket, Pricing, Labor, Lease, Rental, Service, Warranty, Software, and Master
-> Receiving supports PO-based receipt, inspection, rotating asset receipt, and MAS 9.1 return processing
-> Five cost methods (Average, Last, FIFO, LIFO, Standard), dynamic lead time calculation, automated reorder processing
-> Critical gap: there is NO Purchasing RBA yet -- procurement teams must use classic Maximo applications

The procurement backbone is mature. The RBA modernization for purchasing is the biggest remaining gap in MAS 9 supply chain.
```
