## Post Copy

```
Six MAS suite applications form a complete AI-powered supply chain pipeline. Together they transform parts management from reactive to predictive.

-> AI Assist: intelligent search, WO material recommendations, failure-to-parts mapping, document summarization
-> Parts Identifier: photograph unknown parts in the field, get instant catalog matches with stock levels
-> Health: scores drive demand predictability, trigger advance replacement procurement, enable criticality-based stocking
-> Predict: tells you WHEN failure occurs -- enabling pre-positioned parts and elimination of emergency procurement
-> Monitor: sensor data triggers condition-based orders, tracks consumable usage rates, refines reorder points
-> Optimizer: schedules work only when both materials and labor are available -- reducing waste and idle inventory

From sensor reading to parts reservation without human intervention. That is the pipeline.
```
