## Post Copy

```
Every vendor promises predictive maintenance. Maximo Predict actually requires you to earn it.

This is the most data-demanding, skill-intensive application in the entire MAS suite.

-> Four model types: failure probability, predicted failure date, remaining useful life, anomaly detection
-> Data requirements are strict: 20+ failures per mode, 2+ years history, 6+ months sensor data
-> Data science skills ARE required -- you cannot just click Train like Visual Inspection
-> watsonx.ai provides pre-built Jupyter notebooks for common scenarios (pumps, motors, transformers)
-> The chicken-and-egg problem: organizations with great PM programs may lack the failure data needed to train models

The organizations that succeed with Predict walk in with realistic expectations about data, skills, and timeline. The ones that fail buy the license and discover they have seven failures in five years.

Full article in comments. Save this for your team.
```
