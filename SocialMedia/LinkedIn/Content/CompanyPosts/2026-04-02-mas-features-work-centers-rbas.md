## Post Copy

```
Work Centers are gone. Not hidden. Not renamed. Gone.

This is the single most impactful change for daily Maximo users in MAS 9.

-> Work Centers are completely removed -- replaced by Role-Based Applications built on React.js/MAF
-> There is no automatic migration tooling -- every customization must be manually rebuilt
-> Critical gaps remain: no Purchasing RBAs, no Asset moves/swaps, no Supervisor SR-to-WO conversion
-> Technology changed from XML/JSP to React.js -- existing customizations cannot be ported
-> Budget 3+ weeks per complex Work Center reconstruction

If your team customized Work Centers -- and most teams did -- this post is the most important article in the series. Read it before you plan your migration timeline.

Full article in comments. Save this for your team.
```
