- **Supply chain posts** (21, 22, 23, 24, 25) spread across Weeks 2, 3, 5, 6, 7 — never two in the same week
- **AI/ML posts** (10, 11, 12, 13, 14) spread across Weeks 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 — maximum one per week except Week 3
- **Licensing/business posts** (15, 16, comparison) spread across Weeks 6, 8, 9
- **Platform/migration posts** (01, 02, 03, 04) spread across Weeks 1, 2, 4, 7
- **API Collections** posted in Weeks 1 and 9 for bookend developer engagement

### Post Format Consistency
- **Hook:** First 2 lines visible before "...see more" — compelling and specific
- **Body:** Arrow (→) bullets for key takeaways, short paragraphs
- **CTA:** "Save this. Share it with your team." (consistent across all posts)
- **Hashtags:** 3-4 at the END, always includes #IBMMaximo and #TheMaximoGuys
- **No external links** in post body — blog links go in first comment only

### First Comment Template
Every post gets an immediate first comment containing:
1. Blog link: `[BLOG LINK]` placeholder
2. IBM tags: `@IBM @IBM Maximo`
3. Series context: "Part X of our MAS Features series"
4. Engagement question: Topic-specific question to drive comments
5. Additional hashtags: 3-4 supplementary hashtags

### Engagement Protocol
- Post first comment within 30 seconds of publishing
- All team contributors like + comment within 30 minutes
- Reply to every comment on company posts throughout the day
- Comment on 5 other IBM/EAM/Maximo industry posts daily
