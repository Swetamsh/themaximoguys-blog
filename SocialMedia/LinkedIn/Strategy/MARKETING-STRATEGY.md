## 6. Engagement & Community Building Tactics

### Employee Advocacy Program

**Why it matters:** Personal profiles get 561% more reach. In niche B2B like Maximo, your consultants ARE the brand.

**Setup:**
1. Identify 5 team members already on LinkedIn
2. Optimize their profiles (headline: "IBM Maximo Architect @ TheMaximoGuys")
3. Each person posts 1-2x/week from their personal profile, linking back to the company page
4. They comment on every company page post within the first 30 minutes (critical for algorithm)
5. Provide them with ready-to-customize post templates

**Employee Post Template:**
```
[Personal insight or experience]

We just published a deep-dive on [topic] at TheMaximoGuys.

The key insight: [one takeaway in 1-2 sentences]

[Link or "check the comments for the link"]

#IBMMaximo #TheMaximoGuys #EAM
```

### Comment Strategy

- **Comment on 5 industry posts per day** from IBM, IFS, SAP, asset management influencers
- Comments must be **substantive** (3+ sentences, add value, share experience)
- This exposes your brand name to the exact audience you want
- A thoughtful comment on an IBM post seen by 10,000 people > your own post seen by 200

### LinkedIn Newsletter: "The Maximo Insider"

**Cadence:** Bi-weekly (every other Thursday)
**Format:** 800-1,200 words per edition
**Structure:**
1. **The Big Story** — Deep analysis of one Maximo/EAM trend
2. **Practical Tip** — One actionable configuration or migration tip
3. **From the Blog** — Highlight of a recent blog post with key excerpts
4. **Community Spotlight** — Interesting LinkedIn discussion or IBM announcement

**Growth Hack:** Every company post should end with "Subscribe to The Maximo Insider for weekly deep-dives (link in page header)"

### Poll Strategy (1x/week)

High-engagement poll ideas:
- "What's your biggest MAS migration fear?" (Data loss / Customization rework / Cost overrun / Timeline)
- "Which MAS module delivers the most ROI?" (Manage / Monitor / Health / Predict)
- "Your Maximo environment is:" (On-prem 7.6 / MAS 8 On-prem / MAS SaaS / Planning to migrate)
- "Biggest maintenance data challenge?" (Data quality / Integration / Real-time access / Historical migration)
- "When do you plan to migrate to MAS?" (Already done / This year / Next year / No plan yet)

### LinkedIn Live / Audio Events

- Host monthly "Ask a Maximo Architect" sessions
- 30-45 minutes, informal Q&A format
- Promotes both the company page and individual expert profiles
- Record and repurpose clips as short-form video posts
