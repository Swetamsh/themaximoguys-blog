## Content Strategy: The 4-Format Mix

### 1. Carousel Posts (Educational) — 25% of content

**Why:** Instagram carousels get **1.4x more reach** and **3.1x more engagement** than single images. They're the Instagram equivalent of LinkedIn document posts.

**The cheat:** Your LinkedIn carousels can be **directly reposted** to Instagram with minor adjustments.

**Adaptation from LinkedIn:**
| LinkedIn Carousel | Instagram Adaptation |
|------------------|---------------------|
| 1080 x 1350px (4:5) | Same dimensions — no change needed |
| 8-12 slides | Keep 8-10 slides (Instagram max is 20) |
| Professional/clean design | Same design works — Instagram rewards educational content |
| Text-heavy slides | Slightly larger font, more whitespace |

**Example carousels from existing blog content:**
- "7 Things That Changed from Maximo 7.6 to MAS" (THINK-MAS)
- "The MAS Admin Toolkit" (MAS-ADMIN)
- "Predictive Maintenance in 8 Slides" (MAS-PREDICT)
- "Visual Inspection with AI: How It Works" (MAS-VISUAL-INSPECTION)
- "MIF vs REST: The Integration Decision" (MAS-INTEGRATION)

---

### 2. Reels (Short Video) — 60% of content (THE GROWTH LEVER)

**Why:** Reels get **125% more reach** than static posts and **36% more than carousels**. Video content receives **3.7x more engagement** overall. Instagram's algorithm aggressively pushes Reels to non-followers, which is exactly what you need for traction. Every Reel cross-posts to YouTube Shorts.

**Content types:**
| Reel Type | Length | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Quick Tip** | 15-30 sec | "One MAS admin tip you need to know" |
| **Myth Buster** | 30-60 sec | "You DON'T need Cloud Pak for Data to run MAS" |
| **Before/After** | 15-30 sec | "Maximo 7.6 screen vs MAS 9 screen" |
| **Day in the Life** | 30-60 sec | "What a Maximo implementation kickoff looks like" |
| **Explainer** | 30-60 sec | "What is predictive maintenance in 60 seconds?" |
| **Trending Audio** | 15-30 sec | Use trending sounds with Maximo-related captions |

**Production tip:** Film these on your phone. Raw, authentic content outperforms polished production on Instagram by 34%. Talk to the camera or screen-record MAS dashboards.

**Cross-post:** Every Reel can also go to YouTube Shorts and TikTok (if you add that later).

## What Makes Instagram Work for B2B Credibility

### The "Grid Aesthetic" Check
When someone lands on your profile, they see your last 9-12 posts as a grid. This grid IS your first impression.

**Good grid signals:**
- Consistent brand colors and design language
- Mix of carousels, images, and Reels (variety)
- Professional but not sterile (show personality)
- Active (recent posts within the last week)

**Bad grid signals:**
- Random, inconsistent visuals
- All the same format (monotonous)
- Last post was 3 months ago
- Stock photos with no brand identity

### The Follower Count Check
Harsh truth: people judge credibility by follower count. Targets:
- **500 followers** = "They exist"
- **1,000 followers** = "They're getting traction"
- **3,000+ followers** = "They're established"
- **5,000+ followers** = "They're a known brand"

### The Bio Check
Your bio must answer in 3 seconds: "What do they do and why should I care?"

## Instagram SEO (NEW in 2026 — This Is Big)

**Major shift:** Instagram content from business accounts is now **indexed by Google**. Your Reels, carousels, and posts appear in Google search results. This turns every Instagram post into a long-tail discovery asset.

### Caption SEO Checklist (every post)
- [ ] Place primary keywords in the **first 2 lines** of the caption
- [ ] Write keyword-rich, natural language (not keyword-stuffed)
- [ ] Include the topic clearly: "predictive maintenance in IBM Maximo" not "cool new feature"
- [ ] Add a question or CTA in the caption to drive comments (engagement = ranking signal)

### Alt Text SEO Checklist (every post)
- [ ] Go to "Advanced Settings" before publishing
- [ ] Write keyword-informed alt text manually
- [ ] Bad: "Screenshot of dashboard"
- [ ] Good: "IBM Maximo MAS Monitor dashboard showing real-time IoT sensor data for predictive maintenance"

### Why This Matters
- Saves, shares, and watch time now outweigh likes as ranking signals
- Optimized posts surface in Google search results for **weeks or months**
- Someone searching "IBM Maximo predictive maintenance tutorial" could find your Instagram Reel
- This creates a compounding discovery channel that no Maximo competitor is using
