# TheMaximoGuys Email Newsletter Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Newsletter Name:** The Maximo Insider
**Relevance:** 8/10
**Priority:** Tier 1 — Start Immediately

## Why Email Newsletter

- You **OWN your email list** — no algorithm changes can take it away
- Email converts at **3-5x** the rate of social media for B2B
- Pairs with LinkedIn Newsletter (dual distribution)
- Maximo professionals check email religiously (enterprise culture)
