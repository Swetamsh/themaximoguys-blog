# TheMaximoGuys Podcast Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Name:** The Maximo Guys Podcast
**Relevance:** 7/10
**Priority:** Tier 3 — Start Month 6

---

## Why Podcasting

- Only one Maximo podcast exists (Maximo Bite Size / Maximo Secrets)
- Maintenance professionals work in environments where they can listen but not read
- Podcast listeners have **2x higher brand recall** than blog readers
- Builds deep trust and personal connection

---

## Format Options

| Format | Length | Effort | Start |
|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| **Repurposed Audio** | 5-10 min | LOW | Month 4 |
| **Interview Show** | 30-45 min | MEDIUM | Month 6 |
| **Commentary** | 15-20 min | MEDIUM | Month 6 |

**Recommendation:** Start with repurposed audio (read blog posts aloud) — near-zero effort, captures an audience nobody else is reaching.
