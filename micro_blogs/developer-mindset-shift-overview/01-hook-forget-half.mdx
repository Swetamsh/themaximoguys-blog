You've mastered Maximo 7.6. Congratulations -- now forget half of what you know.

That stings, right? If you've spent a decade becoming an expert, the idea of "forgetting" anything feels like an insult.

But here's the thing: you don't actually need to forget anything. You just need to rewire *how* you think about building, integrating, and operating Maximo. The domain knowledge? That comes with you. Work orders, assets, PM, workflow -- all the same.

It's the technology layer underneath that shifts. And that shift isn't a version upgrade. It's an entirely different architecture.
