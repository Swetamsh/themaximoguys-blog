You've mastered Maximo 7.6. Congratulations — now forget half of what you know.

That stings. We know.

You've spent years — maybe a decade — becoming the person your team calls when something breaks at 2 AM. You know MAXATTRIBUTE cold. You can write an automation script in your sleep. You've survived more fixpacks than you care to count.

And now someone's telling you to forget it?

Here's what we actually mean.

Your skills aren't obsolete. Your domain knowledge doesn't expire. Every work order, asset record, PM trigger, and workflow routing you understand — that all comes with you.

What changes is the HOW.

The way you extend Maximo changes. The way you integrate changes. The way you operate and observe changes. The platform underneath your expertise is new.

Think of it like this: you're a master chef who's been cooking on a gas range your whole career. Now you're moving to induction. The recipes are the same. The ingredients are the same. Your palate, your timing, your instincts — all the same. But if you keep reaching for that gas knob, nothing's going to heat up.

MAS 9 is not a bigger, shinier version of 7.6. It's a new platform built on cloud-native principles — API-first, event-driven, observable by design.

The knowledge you've built is the foundation. Now we build a new floor on top of it.
