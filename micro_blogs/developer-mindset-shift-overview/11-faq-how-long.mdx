**"How long does the mindset shift take?"**

Most experienced Maximo professionals report 3-6 months to feel comfortable with MAS patterns.

But there's a catch: the key is *deliberate* learning. Not just doing the same things in a new environment, but understanding why the new patterns exist. If you're just translating your 7.6 habits into MAS equivalents without understanding the reasoning, you'll fight the platform instead of working with it.

The people who transition fastest are the ones who get curious about the "why" instead of just learning the "how."
