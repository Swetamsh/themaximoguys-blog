**"Will my Maximo 7.6 skills become obsolete?"**

No.

Core Maximo concepts -- work orders, assets, preventive maintenance, workflow -- remain the same. What changes is HOW you extend, integrate, and operate the system.

Your domain knowledge is actually more valuable than ever. There's a huge demand for people who understand both the Maximo business layer AND the new technology patterns. Organizations migrating to MAS need exactly that combination.

The specific tools change (WebSphere -> OpenShift, direct SQL -> APIs, MIF -> event streams). The understanding of *why* things work the way they do? That's yours to keep.
