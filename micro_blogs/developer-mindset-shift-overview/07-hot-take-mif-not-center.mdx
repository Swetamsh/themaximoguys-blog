If you're a Maximo integration developer, you probably have a complicated relationship with MIF. Object structures, publish channels, enterprise services -- you could configure an integration in your sleep. And probably dealt with more SOAP web services than you'd like to admit.

Here's the thing about MAS: MIF still exists in Manage. It works. Your existing integrations don't evaporate overnight.

But it's no longer the center of the integration universe. You're entering a world of event streams, API gateways, and tools like App Connect. Batch-first thinking needs to become event-first thinking.

The good news? You can migrate incrementally. You don't have to rewrite everything on day one. But the direction is clear, and leaning into it sooner makes the whole journey smoother.
