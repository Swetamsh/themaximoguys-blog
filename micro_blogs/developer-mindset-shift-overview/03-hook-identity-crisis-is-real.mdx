There's a quiet crisis happening in the Maximo community.

Developers who spent years -- sometimes decades -- becoming experts in 7.6 are watching the rules change underneath them. Custom MBOs, direct SQL, WebSphere tuning, MIF configurations... the things that made you valuable feel like they're slipping away.

They're not. Your domain expertise transfers completely. But the *how* changes fundamentally. And that's an uncomfortable place to be when you've built your career on knowing exactly how things work.

The good news? You've already survived every Maximo upgrade so far. This one's bigger, but you're not starting from zero.
