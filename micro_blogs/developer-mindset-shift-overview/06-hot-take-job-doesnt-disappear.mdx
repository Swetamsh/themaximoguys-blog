"What happens to my job if IBM runs everything?"

It's the quiet fear every Maximo admin has. And it's understandable -- you built your career on owning the full stack. WebSphere, database tuning, patches, 3 AM incidents. All of it.

The honest answer: IBM and Red Hat do handle more of the low-level infrastructure now. But someone still needs to operate MAS within your organization. Manage operators. Configure observability. Handle capacity planning. Understand the shared responsibility model.

That someone is you. Your deep Maximo operational knowledge is exactly what makes you the right person for this new version of the role. The tools change. The responsibility doesn't vanish.
