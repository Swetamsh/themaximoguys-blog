# Modernizing IBM Maximo with AI: A Complete Guide

> **A 6-part blog series on transforming your Enterprise Asset Management with artificial intelligence**

---

## Series Overview

IBM Maximo Application Suite (MAS) has evolved from a traditional CMMS into an AI-powered asset management platform. This series provides a comprehensive roadmap for organizations looking to leverage AI capabilities within their Maximo environment.

Whether you're running classic Maximo 7.x, migrating to MAS 8.x, or already on the latest MAS 9.x, this guide will help you understand what's possible and how to get there.

---

### [Part 1: Why AI for Maximo? The Business Case](./01-why-ai-for-maximo.md)
The strategic imperative for AI-driven asset management. Understand the ROI drivers, industry trends, and why organizations are investing in AI-enabled EAM.

### [Part 2: MAS AI Capabilities Deep Dive](./02-mas-ai-capabilities.md)
