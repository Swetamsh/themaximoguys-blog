## Common Parameters Across Prompts

- **aspect_ratio**: 2:3, 9:16, 4:5, 3:4, 1:1, 16:9, 51:91, 3:2
- **style**: photorealistic, hyper-realistic, cinematic, editorial, tilt-shift macro
- **negative_prompt**: cartoon, anime, blurry, low quality, distorted, CGI look, text, watermark
- **camera specs**: lens focal length (24mm-85mm), aperture (f/1.8-f/8), ISO, shutter speed
- **lighting**: studio lighting, cinematic, natural, flash photography, neon, golden hour
- **rendering engines**: Octane Render, Unreal Engine 5, Cinema 4D
- **film emulation**: CineStill 800T, Kodak Portra 400, Fuji Film Pro 400H
- **identity preservation**: identity_lock, face_preservation, preserve_original fields
- **resolution**: 4K, 8K, ultra-high-resolution
